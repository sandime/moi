SherriCruz

Journalism, amplified by curiosity

Self-taught journalist with more than two decades of experience across reporting, editing, and freelance writing. Technology reporter for the Minnesota Star Tribune. Editor at the Orange County Register, led a team of six reporters/interns, produced two weekly university sections. Career launched by successfully pitching an Internet column in 1996 to the Las Vegas Business Press. Extensive magazine freelance contributor. Author. App developer.
